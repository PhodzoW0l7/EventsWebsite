Behind The Creative Direction

Warren Khosa

Chief Wedding Planner & Creative Director

With a professional background rooted in luxury hospitality, guest experience, and curated travel experiences around private game reserves in the Renowned Sabi Sands, Madikwe Game Reserve, and the Greater Kruger National Park, Warren Khosa brings a unique blend of elegance, operational excellence, and intentional creativity into every celebration.

Having worked within premium hospitality and guest-focused environments, including luxury lodges like Lion Sands River and Ivory Lodge and stakeholder-driven experiences, my journey in hospitality and luxury travel cultivated an eye for detail, aesthetics, seamless coordination, and unforgettable guest experiences.

These milestones became the foundation that birthed Dakalolashu — a brand dedicated to transforming meaningful moments into timeless experiences. At Dakalolashu, every celebration is designed with heart, sophistication, and a deep understanding that life’s most important moments deserve thoughtful execution and beautiful storytelling.
